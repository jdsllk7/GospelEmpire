    ... Music found me when I was about 8 years old I think. I remember I loved to sing as a kid. If there is a stage, audience or mic around then just know I’m there. My dad used to sing in a popular band back in the day and this just made my love for music grow immensely. The problem is that I can’t play any instrument nor can I sing very well but I know what beautiful music looks like. My ears were bred for the sole purpose of music.
